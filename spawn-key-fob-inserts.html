🔑 Spawn Key Fob Inserts — 30 × 13 mm

Sized for the black plastic fobs. Print at 100% / Actual size — never "Fit to page".
Measured from your photo: the current Code128 uses only 58% of the label width, giving a 220 micron narrowest bar. Phones need about 500. That is why it will not focus — less than half the minimum.

Data Matrix at 11mm gives a 917 micron module — 4× larger. It also has Reed-Solomon error correction, so it still reads with the flash hotspot visible in your other photo covering part of it. Code128 has no error correction: one washed-out bar and the read is dead.

The plastic cover is the other half of the problem. Scuff the outside of the cover with fine sandpaper or a nylon scourer — a few passes turns the mirror into a diffuser and kills the hotspot. Or scan with the flash OFF; at 10cm ambient light is usually enough.
